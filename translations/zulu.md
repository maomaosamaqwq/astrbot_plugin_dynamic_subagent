# isiZulu (isiZulu) — Dynamic SubAgent

I-Dynamic SubAgent i-plugin ye-AstrBot evumela i-agent eyinhloko ukuba idale futhi ilawule ama-agent amancinyane ngokuguqukayo. Inamazinga amathathu emvume (safe/medium/full) kanye nemikhawulo yokujula kokungaphakathi. Ama-agent amancinyane awakwazi ukudala ama-agent amancinyane angeziwe, lokhu kuqinisekisa ukuphepha kwesistimu. Isekela inkumbulo eqhubekayo, ukudlulwa kwemisebenzi kanye nokulandelelana ubambiswano. Futhi ixhasa ukuhlanganiswa kwezinsiza ze-MCP kanye nokuphathwa kwempilo epheleleyo.
