# আচিক — Dynamic SubAgent

Dynamic SubAgent na AstrBot-ko pisi plug-in ni, ba man aking Agent-ga saksi Agent-o kama aro kachika chikkaaha na. Miangkang adep gimin isolasi (safe/medium/full) aro nesting gimin chengbakang aha, saksi Agent-o saksi Agent kama kachikkang nai, system-ga adep gimin tachikaha. Daok gimin memory, kachang gimin ka ro aro samasem gimin kachengaha.
