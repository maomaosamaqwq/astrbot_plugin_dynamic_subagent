# 达丹语 — Dynamic SubAgent

Dynamic SubAgent yek plugin-e AstrBot ast ke be Agent-e asli imkan midahad Agent-haye farzandi ra be surat-e dinamik beazad va modiriyat konad. Se sath-e izolasiye-ye mojavez (safe/medium/full) va mahdudiye-ye omq-e tajozozh darad, Agent-haye farzandi nemitavanan Agent-e farzandi digar beazadand, ke amniyat-e sistem ra tazmin mikonad. Az zakhire-ye hadse daemi, enteqal-e vazife va peygiri-ye hamkari pashibani mikonad.

---
*This introduction is written in 达丹语 (Dadan).*
